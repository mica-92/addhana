Bē yno

Nov 1 2017

Micaerys hen Ninnīrio Letrot brōziks. Brōzio tubis lantēpsa tōma iksan. Valyrio muño ēngos ñuhys issa daor yn gūrēñan. Ao Qeptenkys Ēngos temptys iksan. Lanta jaohossa se lanta kēla eman, pōntes Nonnōkerys, Ummērys, Oreys, Torāys brōzan. Pikīnnon se gureñon yne raqiros issi. Muña blog ōtapnoso tubī bardun.

I am called Micaela of House Ninni. I am 25 name days. Valyrian is not my mother tongue but I’m learning. I am a common tongue teacher. I have 2 dogs and 2 cats, I named them Nanook, Umma, Oreo and Tora. I write my blog daily with thoughts.
